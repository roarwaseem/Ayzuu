üìò Comprehension ‚Äì Full Detail

Comprehension ka matlab hota hai:

  • Kisi passage (paragraph) ko parhna
  • Uska meaning samajhna
  • Aur us par sawalon ke jawab dena

Sirf parhna comprehension nahi hota ‚Äî samajhna zaroori hai.

üîπ Comprehension Mein Kya Check Hota Hai?

  1. Tum meaning samajh rahe ho ya nahi
  2. Main idea pakar sakte ho ya nahi
  3. Detail find kar sakte ho ya nahi
  4. Vocabulary samajh sakte ho ya nahi

üîπ Comprehension Karne Ka Sahi Tareeka (Step by Step)

  1. Step 1: Pehle poora paragraph ek baar read karo (Jaldi jaldi nahi ‚Äî araam se).
  2. Step 2: Difficult words underline karo. Jo words samajh na aaye un ka meaning guess karo sentence se.
  3. Step 3: Main idea find karo. Yeh paragraph kis bare mein hai? Har paragraph ka ek main idea hota hai.
  4. Step 4: Questions ko dhyaan se read karo. Answer sirf paragraph se do. Apni taraf se story mat banao.

üîπ Example Se Samjho

Passage:

Ali is a hardworking student. He studies daily and completes his homework on time. His teachers appreciate his efforts. Because of his dedication, he always gets good marks.

Question 1: Why do teachers appreciate Ali?

Answer: Because he studies daily and completes his homework on time.

Question 2: What kind of student is Ali?

Answer: Ali is a hardworking student.

üîπ Comprehension Ke Types

  • Literal Questions: Direct answer paragraph mein hota hai.
  • Inferential Questions: Thora sochna padta hai.
  • Vocabulary Questions: Word ka meaning pocha jata hai.

üîπ Common Mistakes

  • Sirf first line parhna
  • Apni taraf se answer banana
  • Question properly na parhna
  • Time waste karna ek hi question par
